How it works

Three steps to better movement

01
Point your camera
Set up your phone on a tripod or lean it against a surface. No special equipment required, just your phone and the RepRight app.
02
AI analyzes your movement
Our pose estimation model tracks your body in real time, identifying joint angles, movement patterns and form errors as they happen.
03
Get coached and improve
Receive instant corrections, track your form score over time and watch your athletic pillars — technique, explosiveness, endurance — improve session by session.
